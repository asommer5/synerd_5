Foster Children

  • If you are interested in fostering a child, we can begin the process today.
  • We work with fostering agencies to help interested parties find children that may be a fit for their family. This is a serious process that helps to give children better lives.
  • Interested parties can schedule appointments through our outreach program to meet children that are candidates for fostering.

Assistance Programs

  • We have serveral programs available to anyone who are interested in finding ways to help with children in need.
  • There are programs available to help feed, and provide activities for children.
  • Those interested in enrolling in one of these programs can visit our resources page and discover all of the many ways that people are giving help to foster children.

ID Protection

  • We ensure that all anonymous donations remain anonymous.
  • Information used to submit donations is encrypted and protected.
  • Information such as emails, names, and financial information is kept private and will not be used for any other purpose other than processing donations or subscribing to our site.
